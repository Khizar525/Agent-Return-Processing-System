Architecture

Manager + Handoff Hybrid

Tool calls keep context with the orchestrator. Handoffs give specialists full ownership of complex cases.

🧠

Triage Orchestrator

Entry point for all inbound messages. Classifies intent and routes via tool calls or handoffs.

tracking_lookup faq_lookup get_customer_profile
📋

Policy Agent

Evaluates return eligibility against business rules, fraud signals, and customer history.

check_return_policy handoff
🔧

Resolution Agent

Executes refunds, creates return labels, and places replacement orders.

process_refund create_return_label create_replacement_order
💳

Billing Agent

Handles billing disputes and processes refunds with cap enforcement.

process_refund $500 cap
💬

Communication Agent

Sends branded notifications across email, SMS, WhatsApp, and web channels.

send_notification brand voice
🚨

Escalation Agent

Creates human tickets for complex cases and logs resolution outcomes.

create_human_ticket log_resolution

Safety Layer

Guardrails

Automated checks that protect customers and the business at every step.

🔒 PII Scrubber

Detects and redacts credit card numbers, SSNs, and bank account details from all inbound messages.

Scores messages 0.0–1.0 for distress. Legal threats (ALL CAPS + legal keywords) trigger escalation at 0.8.
